Research

My previous research was primarily focused on few-shot learning, where I employed a novel method within the meta-learning framework to address the challenge of limited tasks in few-shot scenarios. Currently, I am enthusiastic about visual language models and large language models. I have proposed a novel approach for text-supervised semantic segmentation using supervision from a large language model, eliminating the need for additional training. Additionally, my research involves the exploration of prompt learning and test-time adaptation.
